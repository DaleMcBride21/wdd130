Content

Home page

Join us for an unmatched hunting experience in Wyoming, where you'll chase big game like moose, elk, mule deer, and whitetail. Our expert outfitters lead you through Wyoming's diverse landscapes, offering hunts that cater to all levels, from beginners to seasoned hunters. We focus on successful, ethical hunting. With us, you're set for an unforgettable adventure, making memories that stand the test of time while respecting the wildlife and habitat of this magnificent state. Let's embark on this journey together, where every hunt is an opportunity to challenge yourself and embrace the great outdoors.

Images for the Home page

About Us

Our crew's just a bunch of Wyoming folks, born and bred in the heart of the wild. Hunting's been our thing since we were knee-high, roaming the hills and valleys, chasing game and soaking in the rugged beauty of our home state. We have always been attracted to the big game such as Deer, Elk, and Moose. But it's not just about the kill; it's about the whole experience. The early mornings, the camaraderie, the stories swapped around the campfire - that's what keeps us coming back for more. Wyoming's our playground, and we'll keep exploring its nooks and crannies, hunting and living life to the fullest. As long as there's game to chase and wilderness to roam, you'll find our crew out there, doing what we love.

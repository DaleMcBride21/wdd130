Elk

This high success hunt for Trophy Elk is in areas 70, 71, and 81. We provide transportation with an excellent team of horses. A comfortable camp, hot home cooked meals and an adventure of a lifetime. Lasting six days we guarentee that you will be able to see some unforgettable country while looking for your dream Trophy. The Price for this hunt is $12500 per person.

Mule Deer

Jackson is a hotspot for Trophy Mule Deer. In areas 148 and 156 we have been able to harvest many deer scoring above the 200 inch mark. We offer transportation by horse, hot meals and a comfortable place to stay. This five day hunt will be one that you will never forget. The Price for this hunt is $9000 per person.

Whitetail Deer

This hunt we offer a four day excursion through areas 148 and 156. Much like Mule deer whitetails are known to get to trophy size. We offer transportaion by horse a high quality camp and hot meals. The Price for this hunt is $5500 per person.

Moose

This hunt lasts a full seven days with transportation with horses, hot meals and a quality place to stay. This is the longest and toughest of our hunts, but it is the most rewarding. Pursueing a large Trophy Moose is one of the best feeling that you can have in life. It is even better when located in Jackson Wyoming. The Price for this hunt is $14000 per person.

Additional Information about these hunts (PLEASE READ)

Physicality of hunts: As an outfitter, we understand the physical demands and challenges that come with hunting elk, deer, and moose. Our seasoned guides know the terrain like the back of their hands, leading hunters through rugged mountains, dense forests, and marshy landscapes in pursuit of these magnificent creatures. Tracking elk and deer requires traversing vast expanses of wilderness. Moose, with their sheer size, present their own set of challenges, requiring hunters to be prepared for thick brush and marshy terrain. Being in the right shape is very important in order to keep you safe and well and upping the chances of you having a successful hunt.

Weather: We as the outfitters cannot control the weather that we face here in Jackson. You as the guest need to be prepared with the proper gear for very cold climates. We suggest a layering system from a reputable brand. We prefer KUIU, but it is up to personal preference. Contact us if you have any questions about gear we recomend!
